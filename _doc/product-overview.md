# Product Overview — Core Truth

> Single source of truth for the product. Early-stage: the concept is confirmed; a few
> details (audience specifics, name, final branding) are still being defined in onboarding
> and are marked **(provisional)** below. Update as those land.

## What it is

A **browser-based five-star resort tycoon / builder game**. The player designs and runs a
luxury resort — building and managing a **five-star restaurant**, a **five-star swimming
pool**, and a **five-star hotel lobby** — serving guests and growing the resort's star
rating over time. It is played entirely in the web browser; no install.

> Note: this is a **game**, not an operations app for a real hotel. The player runs an
> imaginary resort for fun; the product is entertainment, not hospitality software.

## Honest scope (visual fidelity)

The game targets **slick, stylized, polished web visuals** — clean, charming, and
presentation-ready — **not** console-grade / photorealistic AAA 3D. This boundary is
deliberate: the product promises something genuinely fun and shippable in the browser
rather than overselling movie-grade graphics. Stylized 2.5D / lightweight 3D look-and-feel
is the visual north.

## The core loop (what makes it fun)

1. **Build** — place and upgrade the three signature venues (restaurant, pool, lobby).
2. **Serve** — guests arrive; the player keeps them happy (seating, amenities, service).
3. **Rate** — happy guests raise the resort's **star rating**; the rating gates new builds
   and upgrades, creating a satisfying progression spiral.

The product lives or dies on this loop being tight and rewarding from day one.

## Who it's for (provisional)

A **for-fun, hobbyist project**. Working assumption: **casual players who enjoy cozy
management / tycoon games** — pleasant to pick up, satisfying to optimize. Reach intent
(just-me-and-friends vs. anyone online) is being confirmed in onboarding. Treat as casual,
broadly approachable players until specified.

## Tone & brand (provisional)

- **Personality**: playful, polished, aspirational luxury — "run your own five-star world."
- **Feel**: cozy-premium; satisfying feedback; calm, confident, not cluttered.
- **Working palette**: deep navy/teal base with a warm **gold** accent (five-star luxury
  cue) — provisional until the Designer sets final branding.
- **Name**: **(TBD — being chosen in onboarding).**

## Scope / strategic principles

- **Tight core loop first.** The build → serve → rate spiral is the MVP; everything else
  is secondary.
- **Stylized over photoreal.** Lean into charm and clarity, not graphical horsepower.
- **Three signature venues** (restaurant, pool, lobby) are the anchor content of v1.
- **Progression via star rating** is the primary motivator and pacing mechanism.
- **Browser-native, instant play.** Low friction to start; no install, no account wall to
  try it.
