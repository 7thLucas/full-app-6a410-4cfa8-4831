CORE FEATURES

Four roles, one living hotel

Customer VIP Employee Chef
🧑

Customer

A guest. Requests and books the hotel's experiences and services from their own view.

⭐

VIP

A premium guest — everything the customer can do, with priority and extra perks.

🛎️

Employee

Floor staff. Receives guest requests and handles them across the hotel, live.

👨‍🍳

Chef

The kitchen. Sees incoming food and service orders and fulfills them.

The heartbeat of v1: a request flows from Customer / VIP → lands live with the right Employee / Chef → gets fulfilled.
