# Cinq — Design Guidelines

## Palette
- **Base**: deep navy/teal. Suggested: `#0B1F2A` (deep navy-teal) to `#0E2C33` (teal) for backgrounds and surfaces.
- **Surface elevation**: slightly lighter navy-teal panels (e.g. `#13323D`) over the base, with soft borders.
- **Accent**: warm gold — `#C8A24B` / `#E0BC6B` for primary actions, highlights, status accents, and the five-star cue.
- **Text**: warm off-white `#F5EFE3` for primary; muted `#9FB2B8` for secondary.
- **Status colors** (request loop): received = muted gold/amber, in-progress = teal glow, fulfilled = soft green `#5FB28A`. VIP priority = brighter gold halo/badge.

## Typography
- Elegant, high-contrast pairing. A refined serif or high-end sans for the **Cinq** wordmark and headings (aspirational luxury); clean humanist sans for body and UI.
- Generous letter-spacing on the wordmark and section labels. Calm hierarchy, ample whitespace.

## Elevation & feel
- Cozy-premium: soft shadows, gentle gold glows on interactive/active elements, rounded-but-restrained corners.
- Satisfying micro-feedback on actions (placing a request, status changes). Calm, confident, not cluttered.
- Stylized and polished web visuals — clean and charming, not photorealistic AAA 3D.

## Components & layout
- **Role selection / entry**: an inviting "step into a five-star world" entry that lets you pick a role (VIP, Chef, Employee, Customer).
- **Guest views (Customer / VIP)**: browse and place requests/bookings (dining, spa, amenities, service); VIP view shows priority/perks styling. Track live status of own requests.
- **Staff views (Employee / Chef)**: a live incoming queue of requests routed to that role; VIP-priority items surface first with a gold halo; act to advance status (received → in progress → fulfilled).
- Real-time status badges with the status palette above. Clear, warm, premium feedback throughout.
