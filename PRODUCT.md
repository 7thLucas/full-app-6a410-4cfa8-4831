# Cinq — Product

## What it is
**Cinq** (French for *five*, as in five-star) is a browser-based, multi-role five-star hotel experience app. The hotel is lived through **four distinct roles** — **VIP, Chef, Employee, and Customer** — each with its own view and its own actions. Played entirely in the web browser; no install.

## The four roles (the spine)
- **Customer** — a guest; requests and books the hotel's experiences and services (dining, spa, amenities, service).
- **VIP** — a premium guest; same as a Customer, but requests carry **priority and perks**.
- **Employee** — floor staff; **receives and handles** guest requests across the hotel.
- **Chef** — the kitchen; **sees and fulfills** food and service orders.

## The core loop (the heartbeat — day-one MVP)
A live **request/booking loop**:
1. A Customer or VIP places a request or booking (dining, spa, amenities, service) from their own view.
2. It routes in real time to the right staff role — the **Chef** sees food/kitchen orders; the **Employee** sees floor/guest requests.
3. **VIP** requests carry priority and surface first.
4. The request's **status updates back to the guest** in real time (received → in progress → fulfilled).

This guest→staff request-and-fulfillment loop is the heartbeat. The hotel feels alive because someone asks and someone delivers, in real time. Everything else is secondary.

## Who it's for
Broadly-approachable, premium-feeling audience. People who take on one of the four roles — guests (Customer / VIP) on one side, staff (Employee / Chef) on the other.

## Tone & brand
- **Personality**: playful, polished, aspirational luxury — "step into a five-star world."
- **Feel**: cozy-premium; satisfying feedback; calm, confident, not cluttered.
- **Name**: **Cinq** (French for *five* — the five-star cue).

## Strategic principles
- The four roles are the spine. VIP, Chef, Employee, Customer define the whole product.
- Request → fulfillment loop first. That single live loop is the MVP.
- Five-star luxury tone throughout — premium, warm, inviting in every role's view.
- Stylized over photoreal — charm and clarity, not graphical horsepower.
- Browser-native, instant play. Low friction to start; no install.
